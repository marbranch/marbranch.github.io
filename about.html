About Me

I am a twenty-one-year-old college student from New York. Over the past two years, I have gained a deeper appreciation for beauty practices. I started watchng videos online more frequently where self-proclaimed professionals share their talents with the world. I watched everything from nail tutorials to hair tutorials, to makeup tutorials - if it is beauty, I am guaranteed to give it at least a peek. In my consumption of beauty-related content, I have surpassed mere observation. At this point, I have tried many of the beautiful projects that I see online - making my expectations a reality. So, I would like to use this website - "Mariah's Beauty Site" to share my trials and tribulations during my self-teaching beauty school escapades.
